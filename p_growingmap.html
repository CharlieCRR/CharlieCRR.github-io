Growing Map Series

A fan-made trailer for the Second Growing Map.

A community project that I ran over the course of December 2020 and December 2021 for Growing Map #1 and #2 Respectively.

What is the project?
The Growing Map series were two maps I made for Garry's mod, these maps were then published as empty; being devoid of content. The community would then get together and make suggestions for these maps and I would implement them in, and the map would quickly develop over the course of the month into something more.
These maps got REALLY popular and held front-page on the steam workshop for the majority of the time they were active. There have also been a few inspired spin-offs from the original two maps and they have been covered by large YouTubers within the Garry's Mod Community.

What this project taught me
Working Under Pressure: When these maps peaked in viewership and suggestions, I had to work very swiftly to keep up with the demand. It was not uncommon to wake up to 250+ messages overnight and have to sift through them all.
Communication: I had to communicate with members of the community to ensure that I got suggestions executed correctly as per request, but also swiftly to keep up with demand.
Games Design Principals: While this doesn't apply for the first iteration, the second growing map placed suggestions one after another to make a linear story, and it was heavily important that I could direct the player without directly holding their hand in puzzles utilising techniques such as lighting. I would also reward the player for exploring areas off to the side and had to tweak the difficulty of certain segments to make sure it felt right and fair.
