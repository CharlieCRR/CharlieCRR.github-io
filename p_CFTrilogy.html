What this project taught me
Teamwork within a Discipline: While on other projects I've worked with different departments, this was my first time having a tech team that was much bigger one. This taught me about communicating within the team to avoid potential merge conflicts, helping to create and follow a coding standard set by the tech lead.

Professional Practices: Throughout the project we maintained a TO-DO board in which we frequently reviewed and assigned tasks, which was especially prominent in our morning stand-up meetings held within the tech-team. Working so closely to an actively maintained board is a concept that I was familiar with but to enforce it closely to an industry standard was something new but invaluable to learn.

My Contributions
My Main contributions to this project were as follows:

  • Working closely with the tech lead to complete vital tasks and keeping the QA-Submitted bug reports down by troubleshooting and fixing
  • Building the gun and their respective rarity systems up with flexibility in mind for the design team to edit and build upon the base I had made for them.
  • Working closely with design to ensure features programmed were open-ended and could easily be manipulated.
  • Whenever I would have no further things I could be working on, I assisted co-workers within the tech-team.
